The Ford Mustang

Overview

The Ford Mustang classifies at a 2-door sports car. The mustang is arguably one of the most common sports cars sold in America and it has a rich heritage behind it. The Mustang is only sold in RWD and comes in either a fastback or convertible body-style. The MSRP for this vehicle starts at $23,800 and can easily reach upwards of $45,000 depending on what kind of options the vehicle comes equipped with. The Mustang Fastback has five different trim levels: V6, Ecoboost, Eboboost Premium, GT and GT premium. The Convertible has three trims: V6, Ecoboost Premium and GT Premium.

Notable Technology Features

  • 12-speaker Sony sound system
  • 3-person memory seating
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • (DEATC) Dual-Zone Automatic Temperature Control
  • Forward Collision Warning
  • Heated and cooled leather seats
  • Heated steering wheel
  • Lane-keeping alert
  • Parking assist
  • Power moonroof
  • Proximity keyless entry
  • Rain-sending wipers
  • Remote start
  • SiriusXM satellite radio
  • SYNC
  • SYNC with 8-inch MyFord Touch with navigation
